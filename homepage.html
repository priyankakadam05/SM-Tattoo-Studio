Aftercare & Precautions

  • Avoid direct sunlight for 2 weeks.
  • Keep the area clean and moisturized with recommended ointment.
  • Do not pick scabs — let them fall naturally.
  • Avoid swimming or soaking for at least 2 weeks.

What to do if irritation occurs

Mild redness and swelling is normal. For severe pain, pus, or fever — contact the studio immediately.
