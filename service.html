Tattoo Aftercare Tips

Proper care keeps your tattoo vibrant and healthy

Keep it Clean

Wash your tattoo gently with mild, fragrance-free soap and lukewarm water twice a day.

Avoid Sun & Pool

Stay away from direct sunlight, swimming pools, or saunas until your tattoo heals completely.

Don’t Scratch or Pick

It may itch while healing — do not scratch or peel off scabs to avoid fading and infection.

Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of tattoo aftercare cream or recommended moisturizer daily.
