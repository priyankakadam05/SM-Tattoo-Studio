About SM Tattoo Studio

Where Art Meets Skin - A legacy of excellence in tattoo artistry since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by visionary artist Elena Voronov, SM Tattoo Studio emerged from a simple belief: "tattoos are not just ink on skin but expressions of soul."

What began as a small studio in Worli, Mumbai, has grown into a sanctuary for those seeking meaningful body art. Our journey reflects our commitment to Excellence in Artistry and our Client Centered Approach.
